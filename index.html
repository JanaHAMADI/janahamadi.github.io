My Short Bio

After years of studying languages and preparing myself to become a teacher, I stumbled upon programming. Never have I considered a career in IT : gender stereotypes thaught me that technical domains are not for girls.

It was the love at first sight. I found in programming what I loved about languages : studying, learning and using logic to pass a message ot trigger an action. It's creative and challanging work in which you will never stop learning. That's the dream !

I wish to work on products with enviromental issues in mind, to create tools that are light in energy and memory space. I would love to work on products helping to reduce human impact on the climate change.
